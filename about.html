About Us

Benedicto College, a forward-thinking educational institution, constantly seeks ways to uplift Filipinos by providing quality education. In the early 2000s, they identified that education was essential to achieving their mission of fostering better lives for Filipinos. Although overall enrollment rates in the country were high, a significant challenge persisted: attrition rates. Statistics revealed that out of every four students starting first grade, only one would go on to complete a tertiary degree. Recognizing this gap, Benedicto College's leaders saw an opportunity to enhance the nation’s education system. In response, they introduced reforms and innovations, leading to the establishment of Benedicto College in its current form. For more than a decade, the institution has focused on transforming existing educational models to better serve students. Benedicto College begins by strategically choosing schools in key growth areas and reforming their academics, operations, and student environments, aiming to ensure success for young Filipinos, particularly those from low-income families. The college is part of a broader initiative to improve education and expand access to quality learning across the country .

Benedicto College in Mandaue, Cebu, has a strong tradition of academic excellence, with many of its graduates becoming leaders in various industries. The college is also renowned for its deep commitment to community service, running numerous outreach programs and initiatives that aim to improve the lives of people in the Mandaue and Cebu areas. Over the years, Benedicto College has grown to have a lasting impact on the community through its quality education and social responsibility efforts, continuously expanding its reach and influence in the region.
